Short description of the column headers
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
rownr     : row number
deg       : degree of weak del Pezzo surface X
Mtype     : Dynkin type of A(X), where A(X) consists of divisor classes that are associated to the involution M acting on the Neron-Severi lattice.
type      : Dynkin type of B(X) which consist of indecomposable (-2)-classes. The components enclosed with {.} are pointwise preserved by M. The components encloded with [.] are not pointwise preverved but preserved as a whole by M.
degrees   : possible number of outgoing edges at a vertex
labels    : labels of the edges
vert-xfer : vertex transitive
edge_xfer : edge transitive
